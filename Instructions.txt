Part 1
As a store owner, I would like a system that allows me to manage parts inventory. 
This system should let me: 
Add and remove parts from my inventory
Add and remove the quantity of a given part
Show how much I purchased it for and how much I am selling it for
State how much I have in stock
I would like to be able to shut down this program at the end of each day and reopen the system to view past inventory and modify what was saved. 
I would also like to print a report showing revenue by product and the total revenue at my store. 

Part 2
As a store owner, I would like a system that allows me to manage my store inventory and sell items to customers. 
Customers must not be able to access my menus, only theirs.
During a sale to a customer, the system should create a receipt that the customer can review, showing the items purchased and any applicable taxes (7 Percent).
